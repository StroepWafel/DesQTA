# DesQTA Changelog

## [1.0.0] - 2026

### 🎉 DesQTA v1.0 — Your SEQTA Learn Desktop Experience

DesQTA is a modern desktop app that brings SEQTA Learn to your computer with a polished, customizable interface.

**Headlining features:**

- **Native desktop app** — Fast, offline-capable, with system tray and auto-updates
- **AI-powered grade prediction** — Weighted estimates per subject to plan your goals
- **16 customizable dashboard widgets** — Drag, resize, and arrange your perfect homepage
- **Offline-first** — Work without internet; changes sync when you're back online
- **Multi-view assessments** — List, board, calendar, and Gantt views for your assignments
- **Theme Store** — Download community themes or build your own with Theme Builder
- **Cloud Sync** — Sign in with BetterSEQTA Plus to sync settings across devices
- **Focus Timer & Study Tools** — Pomodoro timer, AI quizzes, flashcards, and notes
- **15 languages** — Full localization for international schools
- **Direqt Messages** — Full inbox, compose, attachments, and folders
- **Real-time notifications** — Heartbeat alerts for messages, assessments, and reports
- **QR code sign-in** — Quick login from SEQTA mobile app or email
- **Timetable export** — Export to CSV, PDF, or iCal for your calendar
- **Grade analytics** — Performance charts and trends across subjects
- **Global search** — Jump to any page instantly (Ctrl/Cmd+K)
- **Multiple accounts** — Profile switcher for different SEQTA logins
- **News & notices** — Multi-country RSS feeds and school announcements
- **Plugin system** — Extend DesQTA with community plugins
